Why this year matters

1990 is a hinge year: mall radio, VHS nights, arcade noise, and cable TV still organize daily life, but the decade’s digital personality is already booting in the background.

It feels transitional in the best way — old media is still central, while computers, global news, and weirder screen culture hint at what is coming.
