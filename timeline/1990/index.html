World / culture

  • German reunification closed one Cold War chapter and reshaped Europe.
  • Nelson Mandela was released from prison, a huge anti-apartheid milestone.
  • Hubble Space Telescope launched, giving the decade a new cosmic image machine.

Movies / TV

  • Twin Peaks made broadcast TV feel strange, cinematic, and theory-friendly.
  • Home Alone turned holiday chaos into permanent cable rerun fuel.
  • The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air premiered and made sitcom openings impossible not to rap.

Games

  • Super Famicom launched in Japan, setting up the SNES era.
  • Arcades were still current culture: fighting games, racing cabinets, and high-score initials before nostalgia took over.
  • PC gaming was still a keyboard, floppy, shareware, and manual-reading ritual for many people.

Tech / everyday

  • Tim Berners-Lee was building early World Wide Web components at CERN.
  • Pagers, malls, cable TV, cassettes, and print magazines still organized daily life.
  • The decade starts partly late-80s neon, with grunge/minimalism waiting just offstage.
