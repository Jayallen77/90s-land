Major world / cultural events

  • Hong Kong was handed over from Britain to China.
  • Princess Diana’s death became a global media event.
  • The Kyoto Protocol was adopted.

Music releases + moments

  • Radiohead’s OK Computer, Daft Punk’s Homework, and Biggie’s Life After Death shaped different futures.
  • Teen pop was warming up for full takeover.
  • Electronic music felt increasingly visible.

Movies + TV

  • Titanic dominated theaters and culture.
  • Buffy the Vampire Slayer premiered on TV.
  • Men in Black and Good Will Hunting became staples.

Games

  • Final Fantasy VII helped make cinematic RPGs mainstream.
  • GoldenEye 007 made split-screen multiplayer legendary.
  • Ultima Online helped define early MMO culture.

Tech / internet milestones

  • DVDs launched in the U.S.
  • AIM launched and instant messaging culture accelerated.
  • Blogs were beginning to take shape at the edges.

Everyday life / trends

  • People rented Titanic, quoted movies, and argued over memory cards.
  • Computer rooms became family spaces.
  • Online handles started feeling like second identities.
