Why this year matters

1997 is blockbuster and personal at the same time. Huge movies and games dominate culture, while screen names and buddy lists start making identity feel editable.

The decade becomes cinematic: RPG cutscenes, disaster romance, teen mythology, and early digital habits all stack together.
