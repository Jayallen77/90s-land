Major world / cultural events

  • The Oslo Accords marked a major Middle East peace-process moment.
  • The EU formally came into being.
  • The Waco siege and World Trade Center bombing shaped U.S. news memory.

Music releases + moments

  • Wu-Tang Clan, Björk, Nirvana, Snoop Dogg, and Smashing Pumpkins all dropped era-defining work.
  • Hip-hop regional identities got sharper.
  • Alternative radio became a discovery engine.

Movies + TV

  • Jurassic Park made CGI feel like magic.
  • The X-Files premiered and made conspiracy culture weekly TV.
  • Mighty Morphin Power Rangers hit U.S. TV.

Games

  • Doom made PC gaming faster, darker, and more networked.
  • Myst turned CD-ROM atmosphere into a phenomenon.
  • Mortal Kombat II escalated arcade spectacle.

Tech / internet milestones

  • Mosaic helped make the web visual and browseable.
  • The phrase “information superhighway” started floating around mainstream culture.
  • Shareware disks and computer magazines were discovery tools.

Everyday life / trends

  • Dinosaur fever was everywhere.
  • Kids memorized Power Rangers colors like astrology.
  • Computer labs felt mysterious and limited.
