Why this year matters

1993 turns computers from tools into portals. Visual browsing, CD-ROM worlds, shareware games, and digital effects all make screens feel more mysterious and powerful.

It is the year of the glowing monitor in a dark room: dinosaurs, demons, aliens, and browser buttons.
