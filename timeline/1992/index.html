Why this year matters

1992 is where the early 90s stops feeling transitional and starts feeling like its own ecosystem: more channels, louder games, stronger visual identities, and more media made specifically for kids, teens, and niche scenes.

The web is still mostly background radiation, but cable, home PCs, magazines, malls, and arcades are already training everyone to browse culture by lane.
