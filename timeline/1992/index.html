Major world / cultural events

  • The Maastricht Treaty moved Europe toward the European Union.
  • The Barcelona Olympics gave the world the Dream Team.
  • Los Angeles riots followed the Rodney King verdict.

Music releases + moments

  • Dr. Dre’s The Chronic helped define West Coast hip-hop.
  • R.E.M., Whitney Houston, TLC, and Aphex Twin showed how wide the decade’s sound was.
  • Rave and electronic scenes kept spreading through clubs, warehouses, and flyers.

Movies + TV

  • Cartoon Network launched.
  • Batman: The Animated Series raised the bar for TV animation.
  • The Bodyguard soundtrack became massive.

Games

  • Mortal Kombat hit arcades and sparked debates over game violence.
  • Kirby debuted.
  • Wolfenstein 3D pushed PC action forward.

Tech / internet milestones

  • Windows 3.1 made home and office PCs more common.
  • SMS texting began commercially in early form.
  • AOL-style online life was still emerging for mainstream users.

Everyday life / trends

  • Pogs, slap bracelets, and neon leftovers filled kid culture.
  • Video stores were weekend infrastructure.
  • The mall arcade still mattered.
