Major world / cultural events

  • Nelson Mandela became South Africa’s president.
  • NAFTA took effect.
  • The Channel Tunnel opened between England and France.

Music releases + moments

  • Green Day and The Offspring took punk-pop mainstream.
  • Nas released Illmatic; Biggie released Ready to Die.
  • Nine Inch Nails, Portishead, and Oasis helped stretch the decade’s emotional palette.

Movies + TV

  • Friends premiered and coffee-shop sitcom culture exploded.
  • Pulp Fiction rewired movie dialogue and cool.
  • The Lion King became a Disney/culture giant.

Games

  • PlayStation launched in Japan.
  • Donkey Kong Country showed off pre-rendered console spectacle.
  • Warcraft began building PC strategy culture.

Tech / internet milestones

  • Netscape was founded.
  • Yahoo started as a web directory.
  • Personal homepages and early directories made the web feel handmade.

Everyday life / trends

  • Coffee shops became hangout mythology.
  • VHS collections grew under TVs.
  • Starter jackets, flannel, and band shirts all coexisted.
