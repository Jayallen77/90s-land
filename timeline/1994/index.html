Why this year matters

1994 is when the web starts to look like a place, not just a protocol. Directories, browsers, personal pages, and fan communities begin forming a public map.

Offline, music and film are incredibly rich — punk-pop, hip-hop, sitcoms, and indie-cool all hit at once.
