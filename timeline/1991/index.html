World / culture

  • The Soviet Union dissolved, ending a global era.
  • The Gulf War played out through cable news and changed how people watched live conflict.
  • The World Wide Web became publicly available beyond CERN circles.

Music / scenes

  • Nevermind, Ten, and Out of Time made alternative culture feel less underground.
  • The Low End Theory helped stretch hip-hop’s jazz, language, and album possibilities.
  • Lollapalooza began as a touring festival and future alternative-culture signal.

Movies / TV

  • Terminator 2 made liquid-metal CGI feel like the future had arrived.
  • Beauty and the Beast became a Disney Renaissance landmark.
  • Nicktoons launched with Rugrats, Doug, and Ren & Stimpy.

Games / tech

  • Sonic the Hedgehog gave Sega mascot attitude.
  • Street Fighter II made fighting games a social arcade ritual.
  • Linux began as Linus Torvalds’ hobby kernel project.
