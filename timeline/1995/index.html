Why this year matters

1995 is the moment “online” starts becoming normal conversation. The family computer, the browser, and the CD-ROM all feel like portals.

It is also a blockbuster culture year: Toy Story, PlayStation, Batman Forever, and huge albums make the future feel commercial, emotional, and loud.
