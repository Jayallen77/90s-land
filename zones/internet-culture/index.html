What this zone is about

Personal homepages were identity experiments: tiled backgrounds, favorite-band shrines, pet pages, poetry corners, and “about me” pages that actually felt personal.

Webrings and directories were discovery infrastructure before feeds decided what you saw. You followed a link because another human thought it belonged there.

Guestbooks, hit counters, frames, blink tags, MIDI loops, and “under construction” signs made the web feel handmade and alive.
