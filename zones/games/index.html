1UP Games

Cartridges, arcades, memory cards, LAN parties, and cheat-code folklore

What this zone is about

The decade moved from 16-bit sprites to 3D worlds: SNES/Genesis, PlayStation, Saturn, N64, Dreamcast, and PC gaming all reshaped expectations.

Arcades still mattered early in the decade, while couch multiplayer, demo discs, strategy games, shooters, and RPGs defined home play.

Games became culture objects: soundtracks, playground rumors, magazine previews, cheat books, and rented cartridges with mystery save files.
